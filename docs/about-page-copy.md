## Mission

Relay exists to combine open social identity with private communication and community-owned incentives.

## What Relay Offers

- User-owned social graph and account portability
- End-to-end encrypted mailbox messaging
- DeadDrop broadcasts for scarce, gated information exchange
- Tokenized participation via FARM points and REL utility
- Composable Mini App experiences

## How Privacy Works

Direct messages are encrypted end-to-end by default.
DeadDrops are sealed to intended recipients or rotating channel keys.
Relay infrastructure stores ciphertext, not plaintext.
Burn-after-read mode uses one-time key release and expiry enforcement.

## Profit Model

- Staking and protocol fee participation
- Premium DeadDrop delivery tiers
- Sponsored quests and partner channels
- Tip routing and mini-app revenue share

## Governance

REL holders can propose and vote on protocol parameters, including reward weights, fee schedules, and policy thresholds, through a transparent timelocked governance process.

## Roadmap

- Phase I: FARM engine, REL emissions, DeadDrop MVP
- Phase II: advanced DM features, monetized widgets, NFT utility
- Phase III: partner token programs and expanded governance

## Positioning Statement

Relay is not a closed social platform. It is a protocol-aligned application layer that brings private coordination and incentive design to decentralized social rails.
